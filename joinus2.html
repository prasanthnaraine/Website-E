About US

Nourishment ForNeedy

Agaram is a participative drive, where like minded people come together to help an under-privileged child reach his / her full potential through education. If you are someone who wants to make a difference in the life of a child, you can do that by contributing your time for our various initiatives. Agaram is an organisation that is completely run by volunteers. Volunteers are the pillars of strength for Agaram and they play a key role in every part of Agaram’s initiatives. Many of our projects would not be successful without their continuing support and contribution.
